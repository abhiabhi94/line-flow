# Privacy Policy for LineFlow

**Effective Date:** March 2025

LineFlow is an open-source puzzle game. We value your privacy and have designed the application to collect **no data**.

## 1. No Data Collection
LineFlow does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, including but not limited to:
- Names or email addresses
- IP addresses or device identifiers
- Usage statistics or behavioral analytics
- Geolocation data

## 2. Local Processing & Storage
The game operates entirely on your device. Any game settings, progress, or high scores are stored locally in your browser's internal storage and never leave your hardware.

## 3. GDPR and CCPA Compliance
Since LineFlow does not process, store, or share any personal data, it is inherently compliant with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). We have no user data to share, sell, or lose.

## 4. No Ads or Third-Party Tracking
LineFlow does not contain advertisements, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics scripts (such as Google Analytics).

## 5. Open Source Transparency
The source code for LineFlow is available for public inspection at: https://github.com/abhiabhi94/line-flow. You are welcome to review the code to verify these privacy claims.

## 6. Contact
If you have any questions or concerns regarding this policy, please open an issue on our official GitHub repository.
